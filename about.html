Welcome to the TNEA College Counseling System!

Our platform helps students get into colleges based on their academic scores in a clear and easy way.

Counseling happens in several rounds to fairly assign seats. The admin announces each round, and students are informed if they are eligible. After choosing their preferred colleges, students can check their results once the round ends.

This system is completely free, making it easier for students to access higher education. It uses automated rank calculations and cutoff scores to make the admission process simple and fair.
